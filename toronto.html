University of Toronto

About University of Toronto:

Founded in 1827, U of T is Canada’s leading institution for higher learning, discovery, and innovation, consistently ranked among the top universities globally. It is recognized for its research, multidisciplinary programs, and vibrant city campuses.

Established1827
LocationToronto, Ontario, Canada
TypePublic Research University
Students~97,000 (undergrad and grad)
Campus SizeDowntown, Scarborough, Mississauga campuses
Contactutoronto.ca, admissions.undergraduate@utoronto.ca
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Arts, Science, Engineering, Computer Science, Medicine, Business, Architecture, Education
  • Graduate: MA, MSc, MBA, JD, MD, PhD, professional & research degrees
  • Colleges: Trinity, Victoria, St. Michael’s, University, Innis, New, and others
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: High school diploma, strong grades, standardized test (as required), English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Graduate: Accredited bachelor’s or equivalent, GRE/GMAT as required, research proposal
  • International: Credential review, proof of funds, study permit (visa)
