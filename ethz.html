ETH Zurich – Swiss Federal Institute of Technology

About ETH Zurich:

Founded in 1855, ETH Zurich is Switzerland’s leading STEM university and ranks among the top technical universities worldwide. Known for its Nobel laureates and pioneering research, ETH Zurich is a hub for science, engineering, and innovation in Europe.

Established1855
LocationZurich, Switzerland
TypePublic Research University
Students~24,500
Campus SizeMultiple campuses (main on Hönggerberg)
Contactethz.ch, studienberatung@ethz.ch
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BSc, BEng in Engineering, Natural Sciences, Computer Science, Architecture
  • Graduate: MSc, MEng, PhD, advanced research degrees in all STEM fields
  • Research centers: Physics, Robotics, Materials, Environmental Science, Data Science
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Swiss Matura or international equivalent, English or German proficiency exam
  • Graduate: Accredited Bachelor’s, GRE (for select applicants), research proposal, language requirements
  • International: Credential evaluation, German/English proficiency, application via ETH portal
