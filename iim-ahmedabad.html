Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIM Ahmedabad)

About IIM Ahmedabad:

IIM Ahmedabad, founded in 1961, is India’s top-ranked management institute, acclaimed worldwide for its rigorous programs, innovative pedagogy, and influential alumni leaders. Located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, it is recognized for producing renowned managers, entrepreneurs, and academics.

Programs Offered
  • Postgraduate: PGP (MBA), PGPX (Executive MBA), PGP-FABM (Food & Agribusiness), FPM (PhD)
  • Short-term: Executive education, faculty development, certificate courses
  • Specializations: Marketing, Finance, Strategy, Operations, Analytics, Rural Management, Public Policy
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • PGP/MBA: Admission via CAT, subsequent Written Ability Test & Interview
  • PGPX: GMAT/GRE, work experience, essay, interview
  • FPM (PhD): CAT/GMAT/GRE or other qualifying exams + interview
  • Academic excellence, work experience (for executive), application via IIMA portal
  • International students: admission approved by institute on case basis
