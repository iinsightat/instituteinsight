Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur)

About IIT Kharagpur:

IIT Kharagpur, established in 1951, is the first Indian Institute of Technology. Located in Kharagpur, West Bengal, it is known for academic excellence, research, and innovation across engineering, sciences, law, and management.

Established1951
LocationKharagpur, West Bengal
TypePublic Technical University
Students~13,500
Campus Size2,100 acres (largest in India)
Contactwww.iitkgp.ac.in, admissions@iitkgp.ac.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BTech, BArch, Dual Degree (BTech+MTech), Integrated MSc
  • Postgraduate: MTech, MSc, MBA, LLB, PhD
  • Departments: All engineering branches, science, law (Rajiv Gandhi School of IP Law), management, architecture, humanities
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergrad: JEE Advanced for BTech/BArch
  • Postgrad: GATE, JAM, CAT, or institute entrance exams
  • Academic merit, applications via official IIT Kharagpur portal
  • International admissions available with separate procedures
