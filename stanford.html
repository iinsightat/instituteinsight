Stanford University

About Stanford University:

Founded in 1885, Stanford University is a world-renowned private research university located in the heart of California’s Silicon Valley. Its academic reputation, historic entrepreneurial culture, and global alumni network make it one of the most influential universities in the world.

Established1885

Contactstanford.edu, admissions@stanford.edu
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Liberal Arts, Engineering, Sciences, Social Sciences, Humanities, Computer Science, Business, Design, Law
  • Graduate: MS, PhD, MBA, MD, JD, Masters in multiple disciplines
  • Schools: Humanities & Sciences, Engineering, Earth, Education, Law, Medicine, Business
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Application via Common Application
  • Standardized tests: SAT/ACT (test-optional policy updated annually), TOEFL/IELTS for international
  • Academic performance, essays, recommendations, extracurriculars evaluated holistically
  • Graduate: GRE/GMAT for some programs, online application, interviews
  • International admissions: English proficiency, credential evaluation, financial forms
