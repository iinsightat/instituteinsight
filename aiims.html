All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) New Delhi

About AIIMS New Delhi:

Founded in 1956, AIIMS New Delhi is India’s premier medical institution, hospital, and research centre. It stands at the top of national and international medical education, clinical care, and biomedical research.

Established1956
LocationAnsari Nagar, New Delhi
TypePublic Medical University
Students~3,000
Campus Size~213 acres
Contactaiims.edu, aiimsad@gmail.com
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: MBBS, BSc (nursing, paramedical)
  • Postgraduate: MD, MS, DM, MCh, MSc, PhD
  • Specializations: Medicine, Surgery, Pediatrics, Obstetrics, Gynecology, Cardiology, Oncology, Anesthesiology, Neurosciences, Pathology, Radiology, and more
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • MBBS: NEET-UG exam followed by counseling
  • PG: INI-CET, NEET-PG, or AIIMS-specific exams, interview, and merit
  • Nursing/Paramedical: AIIMS entrance, interviews
  • Applications through AIIMS admissions portal and national exam authorities
  • International applicants: Limited seats; check specific requirements
