University of Delhi (DU)

About University of Delhi:

Founded in 1922, University of Delhi is India’s largest and most well-known public university, famous for its multidisciplinary academic excellence, historic colleges, and vibrant student life in the national capital.

Established1922
LocationDelhi
TypeCentral University
Students~400,000 (across all colleges)
Campus SizeMultiple campuses: North, South, Off-Campus
Contactdu.ac.in, du@du.ac.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BA, BSc, BCom, BBA, BTech, LLB, BEd, and more
  • Postgraduate: MA, MSc, MCom, MBA, LLM, MEd, MPhil, PhD
  • Colleges and faculties: Sri Venkateswara, St. Stephen’s, Hindu, Hansraj, Miranda House, Ramjas, etc.
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Based on CUET (Common University Entrance Test) and eligibility
  • Postgraduate: CUET-PG, DU-ET, or relevant national test
  • Counseling through DU admission portal
  • International candidates: Apply via DU’s Foreign Students’ Registry
