California Institute of Technology (Caltech)

About Caltech:

Founded in 1891, Caltech is a prestigious private research university in Pasadena, California, renowned for its small size and exceptional strength in science, engineering, technology, and space sciences. Caltech has produced many Nobel laureates and has a close association with NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory.

Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Engineering, Computer Science, Earth Sciences, Mathematics
  • Graduate: MS, PhD in all STEM areas; interdisciplinary research programs
  • Very strong programs in Physics, Engineering, Chemistry, Space/Astrophysics
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Application via Common Application or Coalition App, SAT/ACT (optional), TOEFL/IELTS for international
  • Extremely selective, strong performance in sciences & math, essays, recommendations
  • Graduate: GRE (required for some programs), online application, research statement
  • International: English test scores and financial documentation required
