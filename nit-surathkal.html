National Institute of Technology Karnataka, Surathkal (NIT Surathkal)

About NIT Surathkal:

NIT Surathkal (formerly KREC), established in 1960, is a premier engineering institution located on the scenic west coast in Surathkal, Karnataka. It is renowned for high academic standards, research, and beautiful beachfront campus.

Established1960
LocationSurathkal, Karnataka
TypePublic Technical University
Students~6,000
Campus Size295 acres (beachfront)
Contactwww.nitk.ac.in, academic@nitk.edu.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BTech, BArch
  • Postgraduate: MTech, MSc, MBA, MCA, PhD
  • Departments: Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Chemical, CSE, Mining, Metallurgy, IT, Applied Sciences, Architecture, Management
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergrad: JEE Main for BTech/BArch
  • Postgrad: GATE, JAM, CAT, institute-specific exams
  • Admissions based on entrance test, performance & application
  • International students: via DASA/ICC, Office of International Affairs
