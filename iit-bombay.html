Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay)

About IIT Bombay:

Founded in 1958, IIT Bombay is one of India’s top institutes for engineering, technology, and scientific research. Located in Powai, Mumbai, it is internationally recognized for excellence in technical education and research, entrepreneurship, and academic innovation.

Established1958
LocationPowai, Mumbai, Maharashtra
TypePublic Technical University
Students~12,000
Campus Size545 acres
Contactwww.iitb.ac.in, pro@iitb.ac.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BTech, BS, Dual Degree (BTech + MTech), BDes
  • Postgraduate: MTech, MSc, MDes, MBA, PhD
  • Fields: Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Chemical, Aerospace, CS, Materials), Sciences, Design, Management, Interdisciplinary studies
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergrad: JEE Advanced for BTech/BS/Dual, UCEED for BDes
  • Postgrad: GATE, JAM, CEED, CAT, or institute entrance exams
  • Academic records, application through official IIT Bombay portals
  • Foreign nationals: International admissions available, check institute requirements
