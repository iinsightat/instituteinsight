Sorbonne University

About Sorbonne University:

With roots dating to 1257, Sorbonne University in Paris is a historic powerhouse in arts, sciences, medicine, and humanities. The merged modern university is ranked among the top global research institutions with a strong international network and vibrant Paris campus life.

Established1257 (modern merge 2018)
LocationParis, France
TypePublic Multi-Disciplinary Research University
Students~55,000
Campus SizeCentral Paris, multiple historic buildings
Contactsorbonne-universite.fr, international@sorbonne-universite.fr
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Sciences, Humanities, Medicine, Languages, Law, Arts
  • Graduate: Master’s, Doctorate, Professional and Research degrees
  • Historic faculties: Arts, Science, Medicine (Paris Faculties/Departments)
  • International partnerships and French-taught/English-taught programs
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: French baccalaureate or equivalent, language proficiency (French/English)
  • Graduate: Accredited bachelor’s, language exams, research proposal
  • International: Application via Sorbonne portal and “Études en France” process
