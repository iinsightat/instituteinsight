Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur)

About IIT Kanpur:

IIT Kanpur, founded in 1959, is one of India’s top engineering and research institutes. Its sprawling campus in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, is known for strong technical, scientific, and interdisciplinary programs, innovation, and industry collaboration.

Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BTech, BS, Dual Degree, Integrated MSc
  • Postgraduate: MTech, MSc, MBA, MS, PhD
  • Departments: All major engineering branches, sciences, humanities, management, interdisciplinary programs
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergrad: JEE Advanced (BTech/BS)
  • Postgrad: GATE, JAM, CAT, institute entrance exams
  • Selection based on entrance exams, academic record, and application
  • International students: Apply through specific global admissions portal
