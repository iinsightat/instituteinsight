University of Tokyo

About University of Tokyo:

Founded in 1877, University of Tokyo is Japan’s most prestigious university, respected for academic excellence and leadership in research and innovation across Asia and globally. Its beautiful multi-campus setting is home to Nobel laureates, world-leading faculty, and an international student community.

Established1877
LocationTokyo, Japan (Hongo, Komaba, Kashiwa campuses)
TypePublic Research University
Students~28,000
Campus SizeMultiple urban campuses
Contactu-tokyo.ac.jp, public-relations.adm@gs.mail.u-tokyo.ac.jp
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Bachelor’s in Humanities, Sciences, Engineering, Education, Medicine, Economics, Law, Agriculture, and more
  • Graduate: Master’s, Doctoral (PhD), professional degrees across all major disciplines
  • Graduate Schools: Science, Engineering, Medicine, Law, Public Policy, Education, Information Science, Frontier Sciences
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Entrance examination (EJU), Japanese/English language proficiency (JLPT/TOEFL/IELTS), academic transcripts
  • Graduate: Bachelor’s, entrance exam/interview, research proposal, language (English/Japanese test)
  • International: Global courses, English-taught programs, separate admissions for overseas applicants
