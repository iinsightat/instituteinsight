University of Melbourne

About University of Melbourne:

Founded in 1853, the University of Melbourne is Australia’s top-ranked university, renowned for its academic excellence, global research, innovation, and vibrant campus life in the heart of Melbourne city.

Established1853
LocationMelbourne, Victoria, Australia
TypePublic Research University
Students~54,000
Campus SizeParkville campus and several satellite campuses
Contactunimelb.edu.au, study@unimelb.edu.au
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Bachelor of Arts, Science, Commerce, Biomedicine, Environments, Design, and more
  • Graduate: Masters, Professional Degrees, PhD, MBBS, Law, MBA, Research
  • Faculties: Medicine, Law, Engineering, Science, Arts, Business, Education, Veterinary, and more
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Australian Year 12, IB, international diplomas, SAT/ACT, English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Graduate: Recognized bachelor’s, GRE/GMAT (as required), research/dissertation proposals
  • International: International qualifications evaluated, visa requirements for overseas students
