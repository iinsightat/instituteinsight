Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find quick answers to common queries about using InstituteInsight, our features, data, and partnership opportunities. For questions not listed here, please use the Contact Us page.

What is InstituteInsight?
InstituteInsight is a free educational portal providing verified details on leading national and international institutes, covering admissions, scholarships, fees, accommodation, rankings, and more.
How are institutes selected and ranked?
We reference latest government and international ranking bodies (such as NIRF, QS, THE, etc.), considering academic reputation, research output, placements, and student feedback.
Is InstituteInsight free for users?
Yes, our platform is free for students, parents, and educators. We are committed to keeping all essential services and data accessible at zero cost.
How do I apply for scholarships through your portal?
We provide guidelines and official links to institute and government scholarship schemes. Applications are typically submitted through the respective institution’s website or government portal.
Is the admission process explained for every institute?
Yes, each institute profile includes detailed admission criteria, application process, eligibility requirements, and important dates.
Can I trust the data and updates on InstituteInsight?
We strive to keep all information current and accurate, with regular audits. However, please verify critical deadlines and official announcements on the relevant institute’s official website.
How can I contact or collaborate with you?
You can reach us via the Contact Us page for general queries, data updates, or partnership proposals.
How often is your data updated?
We update core data at least annually or whenever major changes are announced by official sources. Significant updates (like rankings, admission windows) are prioritized for timely reflection on the portal.
