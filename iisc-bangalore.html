Indian Institute of Science Bangalore (IISc Bangalore)

About IISc Bangalore:

Founded in 1909, IISc Bangalore is India's premier institution for advanced scientific and technological research and education. Set in a 400-acre campus in Bangalore, it is internationally known for cutting-edge research and postgraduate training in science and engineering.

Established1909
LocationBangalore, Karnataka
TypePublic Research University
Students~4,000
Campus Size400 acres
Contactiisc.ac.in, dean.ug@iisc.ac.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: B.Sc. (Research), B.Tech. in Maths & Computing
  • Postgraduate: M.Tech, M.Des, M.Mgt, integrated PhD, PhD
  • Departments: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Engineering disciplines, Management, Nanoscience, Data Science, and more
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • UG: JEE Main/Advanced, NEET, KVPY or other national exams
  • PG: GATE, JAM, CEED, CAT, or institute entrance
  • Academic excellence, selection through online portal
  • International applicants: Check Office of International Relations for program-specific requirements
