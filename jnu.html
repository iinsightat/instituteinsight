Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU)

About JNU:

Founded in 1969, JNU is a leading Indian public central university renowned for its social sciences, languages, international studies, life sciences, and active research programs, set in a green campus in South Delhi.

Established1969
LocationNew Delhi
TypeCentral University
Students~8,500
Campus Size1,000+ acres
Contactjnu.ac.in, admissions@jnu.ac.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BA (languages), BSc
  • Postgraduate: MA, MSc, MTech, MPhil, PhD, MBA, PG Diplomas
  • Schools: Social Sciences, Life Sciences, Environmental Sciences, International Studies, Arts & Aesthetics, Language, Engineering, Management
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • UG/PG: CUET, JNU Entrance Examinations, national-level scores (JEE/GATE), interviews
  • PhD/MPhil: Entrance test, interview, research proposal
  • Academic merit, application through JNU portal
  • International candidate admissions via the International Students’ Office
