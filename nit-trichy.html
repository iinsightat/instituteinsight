National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (NIT Trichy)

About NIT Trichy:

NIT Trichy, established in 1964, is one of India’s top engineering institutes. Located in Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, it is widely known for its academic excellence, research, and vibrant campus life.

Established1964
LocationTiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu
TypePublic Technical University
Students~7,000
Campus Size800 acres
Contactwww.nitt.edu, admissions@nitt.edu
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BTech, BArch
  • Postgraduate: MTech, MSc, MBA, MCA, PhD
  • Departments: Mechanical, Electrical, Civil, Chemical, CSE, Metallurgy, Architecture, Sciences
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergrad: JEE Main for BTech/BArch
  • Postgrad: GATE, JAM, CAT, institute-specific exams
  • Selections based on entrance exams, records, and application
  • International students: admitted via DASA scheme and others
