National University of Singapore (NUS)

About NUS:

Founded in 1905, NUS is Singapore’s premier university and consistently ranks among the best universities in Asia and the world. It is recognized for its rigorous academic programs, research excellence, and global orientation.

Established1905
LocationSingapore (multiple campuses)
TypePublic Research University
Students~40,000
Campus Size170 hectares (main campus in Kent Ridge)
Contactnus.edu.sg, askadmissions@nus.edu.sg
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: Arts & Social Sciences, Computing, Engineering, Science, Business, Law, Medicine, Design, Environmental Studies
  • Graduate: MSc, MEng, MBA, PhD, advanced research degrees
  • Special programs: Double-degree, joint degree, interdisciplinary studies, research centres
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergraduate: GCE A-levels, IB, international diplomas, SAT/ACT (optional for some), English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Graduate: Recognized bachelor’s, GRE/GMAT (as required), research proposal, language requirements
  • International: Credential review, entrance tests as needed, visa support
