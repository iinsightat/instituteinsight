Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi)

About IIT Delhi:

IIT Delhi, established in 1961, is one of India's premier engineering and technology universities. Located in Hauz Khas, New Delhi, it is recognized globally for excellence in research, technical education, innovation, and entrepreneurship.

Established1961
LocationHauz Khas, New Delhi, Delhi
TypePublic Technical University
Students~11,200
Campus Size320 acres
Contacthome.iitd.ac.in, admissions@admin.iitd.ac.in
Programs Offered
  • Undergraduate: BTech, BSc, Dual Degree (BTech + MTech), BDes
  • Postgraduate: MTech, MSc, MDes, MBA, PhD
  • Departments: Engineering (CS, Mechanical, Electrical, Chemical, Civil, Textile), Sciences, Design, Management, Interdisciplinary areas
Admission & Entry Requirements
  • Undergrad: JEE Advanced for BTech/BS/Dual, UCEED for BDes
  • Postgrad: GATE, JAM, CEED, CAT, or institute entrance exams
  • Academic records, application via official IIT Delhi portals
  • International applicants: May apply through institute’s global programs, requirements vary
